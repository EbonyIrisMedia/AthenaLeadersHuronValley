**Design Movement**: Modern Empowerment Minimalism

**Core Principles**:
1. Purposeful whitespace that creates breathing room and emphasizes key content
2. Strong typographic hierarchy using a combination of elegant serif for headlines and clean sans-serif for body
3. Strategic use of the ATHENA brand colors (navy blue and vibrant orange) as focal points
4. Professional photography showcasing real women leaders in authentic moments

**Color Philosophy**: The navy blue (#1A3A52) represents trust, stability, and professionalism - the foundation of leadership. The vibrant orange (#FF8C00) symbolizes energy, enthusiasm, and the spark of transformation. Together, they create a balance between authority and warmth, mirroring the organization's mission to develop diverse, transformative leaders.

**Layout Paradigm**: Asymmetric grid with intentional negative space. Hero sections use full-width imagery with offset text overlays. Content sections alternate between centered and left-aligned layouts to create visual rhythm. Card-based program displays with subtle elevation.

**Signature Elements**:
1. Diagonal section dividers that create dynamic transitions between content areas
2. Orange accent lines and borders that guide the eye through the page
3. Soft shadows and subtle hover effects that add depth without overwhelming

**Interaction Philosophy**: Smooth, purposeful animations that feel professional rather than playful. Hover states reveal additional information. Transitions are quick (200-300ms) to maintain a sense of efficiency and purpose.

**Animation**: Fade-in on scroll for content sections, subtle scale transforms on hover for interactive elements, smooth color transitions for state changes. All animations respect reduced-motion preferences.

**Typography System**:
- Headlines: Playfair Display (serif) - elegant, authoritative, distinctive
- Body: Inter (sans-serif) - clean, readable, modern
- Hierarchy: 
  * Hero headlines: 48-64px bold
  * Section titles: 32-40px bold
  * Subsections: 24px semibold
  * Body text: 16-18px regular
  * Small text: 14px regular

## Selected Design Philosophy: Modern Empowerment Minimalism

This approach best aligns with the ATHENA brand's professional yet empowering mission. It maintains the established brand colors while creating a sophisticated, modern website that feels both authoritative and welcoming. The asymmetric layouts prevent the generic centered look, while the typography pairing (Playfair Display + Inter) creates visual interest and hierarchy without being overly bold or playful.
